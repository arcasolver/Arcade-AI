Welcome to Pong!

Game Instructions:

Press 1 for singleplayer mode.
Press 2 for two player mode.
Press 0 for AI autoplay mode.

P1 keys: Q (up) and A (down) keys.
P2 keys: P (up) and L (down) keys.

Press Esc to quit the game.
